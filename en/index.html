About Me

Marco Sette

I'm Marco Sette — personal trainer in Padova. Olympic weightlifting, CrossFit, powerlifting, HYROX: my journey led me to specialize in the disciplines that build real strength.

I don't work with generic formulas. Every program I create starts with you — your body, your goals, your starting point. From the beginner taking their first steps to the athlete pushing past their limits.

My Vision
I don't train disciplines.
I train people.

Olympic weightlifting, CrossFit, powerlifting, HYROX — I don't stop at one specialization. I study them all to take the best from each and build the right program for you. The body doesn't work in silos. Neither does my method.
