Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

This policy describes how your personal data is collected and processed when you use the 7 Training by Marco Sette website, in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and applicable Italian data protection legislation.

Note: This privacy policy is a template and does not constitute legal advice. It is recommended to have it reviewed by a qualified professional.

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Marco Sette, VAT registered (Partita IVA), Personal Trainer in Padova.

For any privacy-related requests, you can contact me via Instagram direct message: @marcosette_pt.

2. Data Collected

Through the contact form on this website, the following data is collected:

  • Name (required)
  • Email address (required)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Service of interest (Personal Training or Online Coaching)
  • Message (optional)

This website does not collect browsing data, does not use its own cookies, and does not employ any tracking or profiling technologies.

3. Purpose of Processing

Your personal data is processed exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Responding to information requests submitted through the contact form
  • Providing information about personal training and online coaching services
  • Managing communications related to the provision of requested services

4. Legal Basis

Data processing is based on your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), given through the voluntary submission of the contact form.

5. Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services:

  • Formspree — for processing and delivering contact form submissions. Submitted data is processed by Formspree, Inc. (USA). Formspree Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — for loading the website's typefaces. When you visit the site, your browser makes a request to Google's servers to download the fonts. Google may collect technical data (such as your IP address). Google Privacy Policy.

6. International Data Transfers

Personal data collected through the contact form may be transferred to and processed in the United States through Formspree and Google services. Such transfers comply with the safeguards provided by the GDPR, including standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission.

7. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for the time strictly necessary to respond to the request and provide the service. Once the purpose of processing has been fulfilled, the data will be deleted, unless legal obligations require its retention.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

This website currently does not use cookies or any tracking or profiling technologies. Should analytics tools be introduced in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly and, if necessary, specific consent will be requested.

9. Your Rights

In accordance with Articles 15-22 of the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — obtain confirmation of the existence of your data and receive a copy
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction — request restriction of processing
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to the processing of your data at any time

To exercise these rights, contact me via Instagram direct message: @marcosette_pt.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority (Garante per la Protezione dei Dati Personali) (www.garanteprivacy.it).

10. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be indicated by updating the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about how your data is handled.
